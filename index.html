What this means

Your age of money is the average number of days between when money comes in and when you spend it. The older your money, the more buffer you have.

■ Under 10 days — living paycheck to paycheck
■ 10–30 days — getting there
■ Over 30 days — healthy buffer
■ Over 60 days — excellent cushion
